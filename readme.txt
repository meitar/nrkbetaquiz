Add a quiz about the post that a reader must complete before being able to post a comment.

== Description ==

One of the best ways to ensure your comments are full of great content is to make sure visitors who have actually read your post are the ones responding. This also helps keeps your comments sections are free from harassment, cyberbullying, trolling, and other nastiness such as spam. This plugin lets you add a customized quiz to each post you want to protect.

* You write the question(s) and choose the correct answer(s).
* Readers who already correctly answered a post's quiz won't be quizzed before commenting a second time.
* Boost civil discussion and positive contributions to your comment section.
* Add an extra layer of anti-spam protection to the comments form of your posts.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the unzipped `quiz-commenters` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Edit a post in the WP Admin interface to create quiz questions and answers that will be shown to that post's readers.
1. Save your post as normal. The quiz is automatically saved along with the post.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Why isn't a quiz isn't showing up with my post? =

* Be certain you are looking at a post that has an associated quiz. If no quiz is saved for a post, no quiz will appear when commenting on that post.
* If you've already correctly answered the quiz associated with a post, the quiz will not appear.
